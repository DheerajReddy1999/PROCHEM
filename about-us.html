Who We Are

We are a reputed Engineering Industry specialized in Design, Fabrication, Erection & Commission of process Equipment and Chemical plants. We cater to the needs of Bulk Drugs, Chemicals, Food Processing, Tanneries, Paper Mills, Electric & Electronic Industries, Defence, Automobile and many allied Industries. The company is backed with promoters who have highly experienced in handling heavy tonky projects with the support of technical engineers team.

Our Divisions

Equipment Division

We at "PROCHEM INDUSTRIES" have facilities to design fabricate and supply all types of process Equipments as per ASME, DIN, BS, IS, TEMA & other International Standards. We makes a variety of process equipment all in house and third party inspection namely Reaction Vessels, Storage tanks, Filters, Evaporators, Mixing tanks, Heat exchangers, Pressure Vessels, Material handling Equipment, Scrubbers, Blowers, Fume Extraction systems etc. employing latest manufacturing techniques and in different FRP, PP, PVC, HDPE, PVDF, Graphite and Polyurethine.
